Click a card to select it, then click another card/column to move it. (Simplified rules)

Play Classic Klondike Solitaire

This is a free, browser-based Klondike Solitaire game. Move cards in descending order while alternating colors, and build the four foundation piles from Ace to King. No download, no signup.

How to Play

  • In the tableau, place cards in descending order, alternating red and black.
  • Only Kings can be placed on empty columns.
  • Try to uncover hidden cards and create long sequences.
